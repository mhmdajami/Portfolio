                Solving Geometric Constraint using Graph Method

Geometric constraint solvers based on graphs have become the most popular. The geometric constraint system is graphed, with vertices representing geometric elements and edges representing stated constraints between them.
Graph theory gives rise to graph-constructive solvers. They are based on a study of the constraint graph's structure. The graph constructive technique allows for the creation of reliable and efficient algorithms. For the structural analysis of geometric constraint problems, graph theory provides a powerful idea.
